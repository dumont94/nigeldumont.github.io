2026 · Knockout stage

Road to the final Through the semi-finals

The Round of 32 is in the books — Paraguay stunned Germany on penalties, Morocco ousted the Netherlands the same way, and Argentina needed extra time to shake Cape Verde 3–2. Since then the bracket has burned down to two: Spain and Argentina meet at MetLife on Sunday, with France and England playing for bronze the day before.

2026 · The final

Two left standing Final · Sunday Jul 19

Spain against Argentina at MetLife on Sunday. Spain — group winners who then put out Portugal, Belgium and France without trailing — open as favourites. Argentina, the defending champions, went through extra time twice and needed a stoppage-time comeback to break England. The stakes: Spain's first title since 2010, or the first back-to-back champion since Brazil in 1962.

About this page

A single, self-contained HTML file tracking the 2026 FIFA World Cup: the all-time scoring record, the Golden Boot race, the knockout bracket, the final odds, the record book, and the final group tables.

It's a snapshot, not a live feed. A standalone HTML file can't poll a data feed (no backend, and browsers block cross-origin calls). Every figure marked Live was current as of Jul 15, 2026 — after the semi-finals — and the final and bronze final will change several of them. Group tables and completed rounds are final. A true live version would need a small backend proxy.
